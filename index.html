https://stackoverflow.com/questions/60229970/aws-cli-errorrootcode-for-hash-md5-was-not-found

处理方案: 卸载掉python@2，都2020年了, python3不香嘛

查了一堆的资料,没啥用,重新安装rvm(离线安装)，重新安装cocoapods
